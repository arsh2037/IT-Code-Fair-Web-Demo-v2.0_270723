Junior Minecraft Innovation Challenge

Who can enter

Anyone who is enrolled in CDU IT Courses (VET, undergraduate and postgraduate courses) in Academic year 2022.

Your Mission

Team up with another friend and as a pair, you both will be capturing as many flags as possible in this Cyber Security Challenge hosted by Australian Information Security Association (AISA). You are welcome to participate in person at Casuarina Campus or online.


Limited Seats available so please hurry to register your entrant. We only take 15 teams this year!
